    <h1>Chocolate Mug Cake</h1>
    <p>Indulge in the rich and creamy delight of this homemade Chocolate Mug Cake made in minutes.</p>
  </header>

  <main>
    <section>
      <h2>Ingredients</h2>
      <ul>
        <li>4 tablespoons all-purpose flour</li>
        <li>2 tablespoons cocoa powder</li>
        <li>3 tablespoons sugar</li>
        <li>Pinch of salt</li>
        <li>3 tablespoons milk</li>
        <li>2 tablespoons vegetable oil</li>
        <li>1 teaspoon vanilla extract</li>
        <li>½ teaspoon baking powder</li>
      </ul>
    </section>

    <section>
      <h2>Instructions</h2>
      <ol>
        <li>In a microwave-safe mug, mix together the flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, and salt.</li>
        <li>Add the milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract. Stir well until smooth and lump-free.</li>
        <li>Microwave on high for 70–90 seconds (watch closely so it doesn’t overflow).</li>
        <li>Let it cool for about a minute.</li>
        <li><em>Optional:</em> Top with vanilla ice cream, whipped cream, or chocolate chips for extra indulgence.</li>
      </ol>
    </section>
